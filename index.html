About AngelWorx

Leslie Jones

Founder & Producer

AngelWorx is a South African film and animation production company dedicated to telling authentic stories that matter. Founded by Leslie Jones, we specialize in bringing powerful narratives to life through film, television, and animation.

Our mission is to create content that challenges perspectives, celebrates culture, and pushes creative boundaries.
